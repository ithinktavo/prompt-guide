General Performance Achievement

All levels

What can help me achieve my best performance?


There are five key actions to take to help you achieve your best performance:

Maximizing your personal impact by understanding your strengths and how you apply them.
Getting clear on what you must achieve in the next few months and make them your priority every day.
Owning your success by asking for and sharing feedback that is frequent, descriptive and authentic.
Taking personalized actions to grow, which will enable you to make continuous progress on your goals.
Engage with your team by defining ways your team will apply strengths, align on priorities and enhance engagement.
