Metric

AI Tool Usage

Description
This metric refers to your AI tool usage or usage in groups you oversee. This can be measured by your frequency of usage, level of mastery, number of tools used, etc. (For your own AI proficiency and skills, consider the "AI Proficiency / Skills" metric. For ML6 and up, for the AI proficiency of groups you oversee, consider the "AI Proficiency and Adoption (Team)" metric.)
